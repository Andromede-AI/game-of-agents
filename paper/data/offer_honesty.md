# Offer Description Honesty — Case Study Material

Generated by `analysis/offer_honesty.py`. For each offer, we extract claimed rank and claimed rating from the title and compare against the seller's actual state at the time of listing.

**Verdict definitions:**
- `supported`: claim matches actual state within slack
- `borderline`: claim is 1-off or within +5 rating slack
- `false`: claim is demonstrably inconsistent with actual state
- `no_numeric_claims`: title makes no verifiable numeric claims

**Slack:** rank claims are evaluated against FINAL tournament rank (conservative: we may miss transient truths); rating claims allow +2.0 mu exact-match and +5.0 mu borderline.
